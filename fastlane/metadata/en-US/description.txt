LiteMonk is a subtle and beautiful companion designed directly for your macOS menu bar, bringing peace, calm, and mindfulness into your daily workspace.

Without any complex routines or demanding schedules, LiteMonk helps you cultivate presence naturally through its minimalist, zen-inspired features:

KEY FEATURES:
• Floating Zen Pet: A cute, non-intrusive animated character that floats on your desktop. Whenever you feel overwhelmed or need a break, simply click on the character to hear a soothing temple block sound.
• Mindfulness Bell: Set a gentle bell to chime every 30 minutes, 1 hour, or 2 hours, prompting you to pause, breathe, and relax your body. Supports customizable "Quiet Hours" so it never interrupts you during meetings or focus blocks.
• Dhammapada Quotes: Beautifully displayed verses from the Dhammapada appear dynamically on your screen, offering timeless wisdom and positive reflections throughout your day.
• Clean Personalization: Easily adjust character size, text font sizes, sound volumes, and quiet hours to fit seamlessly into your workspace.
• Premium Minimalist Design: Built natively with Swift and SwiftUI, supporting gorgeous Light and Dark modes that blend perfectly into macOS.

Bring a touch of tranquility to your desktop. Let LiteMonk be the gentle reminder you need to return to yourself.
